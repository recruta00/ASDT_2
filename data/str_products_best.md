## What the top performers have in common

- **Bedrooms:** high earners cluster at 4BR (12/15 of the overall top 15); larger 3-4BR units dominate revenue, capturing group/family stays at premium ADR.
- **Villas with pools:** 15/15 of the overall top revenue leaders are villas/homes (vs apartments); listings advertising a **private pool** and villa layout command the highest TTM revenue despite lower occupancy.
- **District:** revenue concentrates in premium/beach-and-central districts (Da Nang, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City); location near beach (Da Nang) or central D1/D7 (HCMC) is the strongest revenue driver.
