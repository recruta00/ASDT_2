## RECITALS / CĂN CỨ

WHEREAS:

(A) The Owner is the lawful holder of the land-use rights and owner of the house/villa described in **Schedule 1** (the **"Property"**), which is an **individual house / villa / homestay (*nhà ở riêng lẻ*)** and **not a condominium apartment (*căn hộ chung cư*)**;

(B) The Operator wishes to lease the Property to operate a lawful **short-term tourist-accommodation business (*kinh doanh dịch vụ lưu trú du lịch*)**, sub-letting the Property to a rotating pool of short-stay guests via online travel platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com and similar OTAs);

(C) Under the **Civil Code 2015 (Law No. 91/2015/QH13), Article 475**, a lessee may sublease the leased property **only with the lessor's consent**, and under **Article 480** the lessee must use the property for the agreed purpose; the Parties therefore wish to record the Owner's express consent to subleasing and to commercial-lodging use; and

(D) The Parties acknowledge this Agreement should be reviewed by a licensed Vietnamese lawyer and notarized before it is relied upon.

NOW THEREFORE the Parties agree as follows.

## CLAUSE 1 — DEFINITIONS / GIẢI THÍCH TỪ NGỮ

1.1 **"Property"** — the house/villa and land described in **Schedule 1**, together with fixtures listed in the handover inventory (**Schedule 2**).

1.2 **"STR" / "Short-Term Rental"** — the sub-letting of the Property (whole or by room) to guests on a nightly, daily, weekly or other short-stay basis for tourist-accommodation purposes.

1.3 **"Guest"** — any short-stay sub-tenant, named or unnamed, booking through an OTA or directly.

1.4 **"OTA"** — online travel agency / booking platform, including Airbnb, Booking.com, Agoda, and similar.

1.5 **"Fit-Out"** — the furnishings, equipment, smart locks, keyboxes, signage, décor and non-structural improvements installed by the Operator (see Clause 12).

1.6 **"Licences"** — collectively the business registration, security-and-order eligibility certificate (**ANTT**), fire-prevention (**PCCC**) compliance, tourism-accommodation notification, and any other permit required to operate STR lawfully at the Property.

1.7 **"VND"** — Vietnamese Dong, the currency of all payments under this Agreement.

1.8 **"Commencement Date"** — **[DD/MM/YYYY]**.

## CLAUSE 3 — PERMITTED USE (COMMERCIAL LODGING) / MỤC ĐÍCH SỬ DỤNG

3.1 The permitted use of the Property is **tourist-accommodation business (*kinh doanh dịch vụ lưu trú du lịch*)**, expressly including short-term rental to Guests, together with incidental residential use by the Operator's staff/caretaker.

3.2 The Owner acknowledges this is a **commercial, income-generating use** — not "residential only" — and consents to it under **Civil Code Article 480**. The Operator's operation of an STR business is not, and shall not be treated as, a breach of permitted use.

3.3 The Operator shall operate lawfully and shall not use the Property for any activity prohibited by Vietnamese law (e.g. unlawful goods, gambling, activities harmful to security/order, excessive noise or pollution disturbing neighbors).

## CLAUSE 4 — SUBLEASE / STR GRANT (CORE CLAUSE) / QUYỀN CHO THUÊ LẠI

**⭐ This is the single most important clause. Without it the entire business model is unlawful under Civil Code Article 475.**

4.1 Pursuant to **Civil Code 2015 (Law No. 91/2015/QH13), Article 475**, the Owner hereby gives **express, standing, irrevocable and transferable consent** for the Operator to **sub-let the Property, in whole or in part, to a rotating and unnamed pool of short-stay Guests** on a nightly/daily/weekly basis via OTAs or directly, **for the entire Term and any renewal**.

4.2 This consent is **not** a one-off consent to a single named subtenant. It is a **general, continuing authorization** covering repeated short-term subleases to Guests whose identities are not known in advance.

4.3 The Owner shall not withdraw, condition, or narrow this consent during the Term, and shall not require prior approval of individual Guests or bookings.

4.4 The Operator sets Guest rates, booking terms and house rules at its sole discretion, and retains all STR revenue.
> **[VERIFY WITH LAWYER]** Vietnamese courts read consents narrowly. Have counsel draft Clauses 4.1–4.2 so the consent is unambiguously a standing consent to repeated, unnamed short-term subleases — this is the clause generic residential leases silently omit.

5.2 The Owner shall provide, on request, notarized copies of the **Land Use Rights & House Ownership Certificate**, the Owner's **CCCD/ID**, and any other title or identity document required by an authority for the Licences.

## CLAUSE 16 — DEFAULT, TERMINATION & COMPENSATION / VI PHẠM, CHẤM DỨT & BỒI THƯỜNG

16.1 **Owner's limited grounds.** The Owner may terminate early only for a material Operator breach (e.g. rent unpaid **[30+] days** after notice; unlawful use) that remains **uncured after [30] days'** written notice (**cure right**). References: **Civil Code Articles 428 and 480**.

16.2 **Operator's grounds.** The Operator may terminate if the Owner breaches Clauses 3, 4, 5, 6, 15, or 17 and fails to cure within **[30] days**, or if a Licence cannot be obtained/kept due to the Owner's default or a title/land defect.

16.3 **Compensation for Owner's early termination or breach.** If the Owner terminates early other than for the Operator's uncured default, or breaches such that the Operator must terminate, the Owner shall compensate the Operator for:
- (a) the **unamortized Fit-Out** cost (straight-line over the Term);
- (b) **committed forward bookings** the Operator must refund/rebook; and
- (c) reasonable relocation/deregistration costs;
in addition to return of the deposit under Clause 11.

16.4 **Deposit forfeiture** applies only where the Operator is the uncured defaulting Party, and is limited to the Owner's actual proven loss and any contractual penalty lawful under Vietnamese law.

16.5 Termination does not release accrued obligations. Surviving clauses: 11, 12, 14, 16, 20, 26.
