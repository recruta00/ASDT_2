## 3. LANDLORD CONSENTS GRANTED

The Landlord (and every co-owner in §1.2) hereby grants the following consents to the Tenant, **effective for the entire remaining term of the Main Lease and any renewal**:

### 3.1 Consent to short-term subletting (*Đồng ý cho thuê lại ngắn hạn*)
The Landlord **irrevocably consents** to the Tenant sub-letting the Property, in whole or in part, to a **rotating pool of unnamed short-stay guests** (nightly, daily, weekly or monthly) — including bookings made through online platforms such as **Airbnb, Booking.com, Agoda and similar OTAs**.
- This is a **standing, continuing consent**, not a one-off consent to a single named subtenant.
- It satisfies **Civil Code 2015 (Law No. 91/2015/QH13), Art. 475** ("*Bên thuê có quyền cho thuê lại tài sản mà mình đã thuê, nếu được bên cho thuê đồng ý*") — the lessee may sublease only if the lessor agrees.
- The **permitted use** of the Property is hereby confirmed to include **tourist-accommodation business (*kinh doanh dịch vụ lưu trú du lịch*)**, and is **not limited to residential-only use** (Civil Code Art. 480).

### 3.2 Consent to use of the address (*Đồng ý sử dụng địa chỉ*)
The Landlord consents to the Tenant using the Property's address as:
- **(a)** the **registered business address / business location** for the Tenant's household business (*hộ kinh doanh*) or company, and for the **Law on Tourism notification** (Law No. 09/2017/QH14, Art. 49) to the provincial tourism authority (*Sở Văn hóa, Thể thao và Du lịch*); and
- **(b)** the **registered accommodation address** for mandatory guest stay-notification / temporary-residence declarations:
  - Vietnamese guests — *thông báo lưu trú*, Law on Residence 2020 (Law No. 68/2020/QH14), Art. 30;
  - Foreign guests — temporary-residence declaration, Law No. 47/2014/QH13, Art. 33 (within **12 hours** of arrival), filed by the Tenant as accommodation operator.
- The Landlord agrees the **Tenant is the accommodation operator** responsible for these filings.

### 3.3 Consent to furnishing & minor works (*Đồng ý trang bị nội thất và sửa chữa nhỏ*)
The Landlord consents to the Tenant, at the Tenant's cost:
- furnishing and equipping the Property (furniture, appliances, linens, décor);
- installing **smart locks, key-boxes, Wi-Fi, cameras at common/exterior points (not private interior rooms), signage** and safety equipment (smoke/fire alarms, extinguishers, first-aid);
- making **non-structural** alterations and cosmetic works (painting, fixtures, partitions that are not load-bearing).
- **Structural changes** require the Landlord's separate written approval. The Tenant **owns all movable items** it installs and may remove them at end of term; the Tenant is **not** required to restore cosmetic improvements the Landlord elects to keep. Make-good scope: **[DESCRIBE / "return in tenantable condition, fair wear and tear excepted"]**.

## 4. LANDLORD COOPERATION ON LICENSING (*Hợp tác cấp phép*)

So the Tenant can operate lawfully, the Landlord agrees to **sign and provide, promptly and without extra charge**, documents reasonably required for:
- **(a)** household-business / company registration (fee typically **≤ VND 150,000**; many localities exempt it);
- **(b)** the security & order certificate (*Giấy chứng nhận đủ điều kiện về ANTT*) — **Decree 96/2016/NĐ-CP as amended by Decree 56/2023/NĐ-CP**;
- **(c)** fire-prevention compliance (*PCCC*) — **Law No. 55/2024/QH15** (in force 1 July 2025) and its current guiding decree; **[VERIFY-PRO]**
- **(d)** the Law on Tourism Art. 49 accommodation notification.

The Landlord **warrants** that the land-use purpose is **residential land (*đất ở*)**, that construction is lawful with no unpermitted extensions, and that the Landlord has authority to grant every consent above. **[VERIFY-PRO]**
