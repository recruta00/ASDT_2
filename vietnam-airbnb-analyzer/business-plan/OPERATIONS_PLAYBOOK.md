## 2. Distribution Strategy (who books where)

- **Domestic Vietnamese guests** (your core weekend/Tet market) now book on the same OTA stack as foreigners, but skew to **Agoda + Traveloka + Booking.com** and pay by **wallet (MoMo/ZaloPay/VNPay)**, not card. List on all three.
- **Airbnb** = keep for foreign guests and premium villa positioning, but do **not** rely on it alone for weekday fill.
- **Direct / commission-free** is your margin lever given the net-negative economics: build a **Facebook fanpage**, join Da Lat homestay/review groups, and run a **Zalo OA** for repeat guests and Tet pre-sells. A meaningful share of Vietnamese homestay bookings still close over Zalo/Facebook Messenger with 0% commission.
- Connect every OTA through **one channel manager** (Hostaway or ezCloud) to avoid double-bookings across this multi-platform spread — non-negotiable with 3 units on 5+ channels.

## 3. Da Lat Pricing Strategy (the core viability lever)

Da Lat is **domestic, weekend- and holiday-driven**, with weak weekday demand (market avg occ ~39%). During Tet 2026, Lam Dong ran 75-85% occupancy (85-95% for 3-5 star) and **room rates tripled**. Da Lat was Vietnam's most-searched Tet destination (+44% YoY searches in 2025). Your pricing must be steeply calendar-tiered, not flat.

**Recommended weekend-vs-weekday ratio for Da Lat: target a Saturday rate ~1.6-2.0× your weekday base.** Concretely:

| Night type | Multiplier vs weekday base | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Mon-Thu (weekday) | **1.0× (base)** | Discount aggressively; this is the trough |
| Friday | **1.3-1.6×** | Domestic weekenders arrive Fri PM |
| Saturday | **1.6-2.0×** | Peak demand night — push hardest here |
| Sunday | **0.9-1.1×** | Soft; often check-out day |
| Public holidays (30 Apr-1 May, 2 Sep, solar NY) | **2.0-2.5×** | 2-3 night min-stay |
| **Tet (Lunar New Year, ~7-10 days)** | **2.5-3.0×** | 3-4 night min-stay; open calendar & pre-sell 3-4 months out via Zalo/Facebook |
| Christmas / Valentine's / flower festival | **1.8-2.5×** | Da Lat-specific romance/flower-festival spikes |

Tactics:
- **Villas positioned premium** (Xuan Huong 3BR, Hillside 4BR): anchor toward the best-operator **~$98 ADR (≈2.55M VND)** on weekends/holidays — professional photos + party/BBQ/group-friendly framing (weekend group getaways are the Da Lat use case).
- **Fill the weekday trough**, don't chase rate: enable **weekly/monthly discounts (15-30%)** and market to remote workers/long-stay domestic guests; set **last-minute / orphan-gap discounts** in PriceLabs.
- **Min-stay rules**: 2 nights on weekends, 3-4 on Tet/holidays; keep 1-night open on weekdays to catch any demand.
- Let **PriceLabs** automate all of the above off a base price + Da Lat market data; hand-override Tet/festival dates upward.
- **Deposit to reduce domestic no-shows**: collect a VietQR/MoMo deposit for direct/OTA pay-at-property bookings.

## 4. Launch Ops Checklist (do before going live — ~first 30-45 days)

**Legal / registration (front-load — [VERIFY-PRO] all of this with a licensed VN lawyer/accountant):**
- [ ] Confirm business registration + tourist-accommodation conditional business line and whether foreign ownership is permitted for this activity. **[VERIFY-PRO]**
- [ ] Confirm tax registration, VAT, and PIT/CIT treatment of STR revenue and Airbnb foreign-source payouts. **[VERIFY-PRO]**
- [ ] Set up **guest residence declaration** process with Ward police (Vietnamese guests) and immigration declaration for foreign guests. This is a real, enforced operational duty — confirm the exact filing method with a lawyer. **[VERIFY-PRO]**
- [ ] Fire/safety and building-use conditions for each sublet unit; confirm landlord written consent to sublet on the head-lease. **[VERIFY-PRO]**

**Payments:**
- [ ] Open VND business bank account; set up **VietQR** (accepts MoMo/ZaloPay/VNPay/ShopeePay via one QR).
- [ ] Register **MoMo and/or VNPay merchant** profile for deposits + direct bookings.
- [ ] Set up **Payoneer or Wise** to receive Airbnb USD payouts and convert to VND. **[VERIFY-PRO]** (compliance of foreign-currency inflow).

**Tech stack:**
- [ ] Sign up channel manager (Hostaway or ezCloud) — connect Airbnb, Booking.com, Agoda, Traveloka.
- [ ] Sign up **PriceLabs**, set base prices + weekend/Tet multipliers per Section 3.
- [ ] Install **smart locks** (TTLock/Tuya) on all 3 units; test remote code generation.
- [ ] Create **Facebook fanpage**, **Zalo OA**, **Google Business Profile**; join Da Lat homestay groups.

**Listing prep (per unit):**
- [ ] Professional photo shoot (villas first — this drives the premium ADR).
- [ ] Write bilingual (VN + EN) listings; emphasize view, group/party capacity, weekend-getaway framing.
- [ ] Stock **welcome kit + consumables**: water, Da Lat coffee/tea, toiletries, coffee filter (phin), local snacks; house manual in VN/EN.
- [ ] Set up linen par-stock (2-3 sets/bed) + towels; contract a **local laundry** (kg-based) and **bTaskee** for turnovers.
- [ ] Pre-write **auto-messages** (booking confirm, check-in code, mid-stay check, checkout, review request) in VN + EN.

## 5. Monthly Operating Checklist (realistic, recurring)

**Weekly:**
- [ ] Review PriceLabs pricing vs. next 30-60 days; hand-tune upcoming weekends, festivals, holidays.
- [ ] Confirm all upcoming turnovers scheduled with bTaskee/cleaner; laundry linen count OK.
- [ ] Respond to all OTA + Zalo/Facebook inquiries within ~1 hour (response time affects OTA ranking).
- [ ] Send/confirm smart-lock codes for arriving guests; verify auto-messages fired.
- [ ] File guest residence/immigration declarations for the week's arrivals. **[VERIFY-PRO]** on method.

**Per turnover:**
- [ ] Cleaner checklist (bathroom, kitchen, linens, restock consumables, damage/inventory check).
- [ ] Photo-verify readiness before next check-in; re-stock welcome kit.

**Monthly:**
- [ ] Reconcile revenue across all channels + payouts (Airbnb USD, OTA VND, direct VietQR/MoMo).
- [ ] Pay channel-manager, PriceLabs, cleaning, laundry, consumables, landlord rent; log against per-unit P&L.
- [ ] Pull each unit's **occupancy + ADR vs. target** (villa target ~$98 ADR / ~50% occ; watch weekday drag).
- [ ] Request/chase reviews on all channels; respond to every review (esp. Vietnamese-language ones).
- [ ] Pre-sell next big peak (weekends 4-6 wks out, Tet/holidays 3-4 months out) via Zalo/Facebook to repeat guests.
- [ ] Preventive maintenance sweep (heating, hot water — Da Lat is cold; wifi; smart-lock batteries).
- [ ] Restock inventory bulk buys (toiletries, coffee, water); reconcile linen losses.
- [ ] Review negotiated rent progress toward max-supportable levels — the business is net-negative at asking rents, so track this monthly as the #1 viability metric.
